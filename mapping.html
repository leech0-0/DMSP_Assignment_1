Mapping

The spacebar is the only input needed for holdspace and is incredibly responsive regardless of the duration the spacebar was held for. The vast contrast from a pure black interface (with the exception of the 3D prism that occasionally appears when the soundscape is rewinding) to a blindingly white space is an effective way for the developer to provide visual feedback for the user.

In comparison, the main source of UI mapping in orb.farm would be the scale representing CO2 and O2, however this is not as well-explained to the user as the documented help does not include the importance of the scale and what the implications could be for the created environment if the scale was to tip too far into each section.
